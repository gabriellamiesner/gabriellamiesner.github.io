about

i am currently studying computer science at missouri s & t. this past summer, i was a kode with klossy web development scholar and a student at google's computer science summer institute. i am a 2020 graduate of launchcode's codergirl program, where i learned java and javascript.


my skills are most developed in the realm of web development, but i am always curious to learn more about everything relating to tech and social change. i believe tech has an immense power for social change, and want to be a part of the movement that will transform the lives of everyone around the planet for the better.


when you don't find me typing away on my laptop or studying, you'll be able to easily find me listening to endless hours of podcasts, on about everything from income inequality to baboons. i love to create things, whether that be with my hands or keystrokes, so you can also often find me painting or creating digital art.
